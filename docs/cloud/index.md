## What is Hangar Cloud?

Hangar Cloud is the managed governance platform for MCP Hangar. It adds centralized visibility, team collaboration, and enterprise compliance on top of the open-source [mcp-hangar agent](/docs/oss/).

- **📊 Fleet Dashboard** — See every agent, provider, and tool call across your organization
- **🔍 Audit Trail** — Identity-aware, queryable, with CEF compliance export
- **👥 Team Management** — Seats, roles, and invitations
- **🔐 Policy Engine** — Centralized policies pushed to agents in real time
- **📈 SIEM Integrations** — Splunk, Datadog, Microsoft Sentinel
